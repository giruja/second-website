About Us

Perfumes are complex mixtures of fragrant essential oils or aroma compounds, fixatives, and solvents, typically in liquid form, used to give the human body, animals, food, objects, and living-spaces a pleasant scent. The art of perfumery dates back to ancient Mesopotamia and Egypt, and it has evolved significantly through the ages into a multi-billion dollar industry
